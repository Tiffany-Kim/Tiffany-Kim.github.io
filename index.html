About

Hi there!

I graduated from the University of Toronto in 2020 with an Honours Bachelor of Arts Degree in Public Health and International Relations. As someone terribly afraid of hospitals and white gowns, I never thought I would end up pursuing a career in healthcare. I was halfway into my degree when I stumbled into a Health Policy course and realized that there was a way to influence the healthcare system from beyond the hospital and that — I absolutely loved. Since then, I have worked jobs in pharmaceutical marketing and advertising, research, and cancer care. During my free time, I like to write, visit new restaurants to share on my food instagram, and challenge myself to live a more meaningful, authentic life. To be honest, I’m still figuring it out!
